THE VILLAGE
Competitive Analysis
How The Village compares to Peanut, What to Expect, BabyCenter, Ovia, and others

Prepared for feature development briefing | Miami, FL | April 2026
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Village targets an underserved gap: a local-first, bilingual, community-driven app for mothers in Miami — with unique features no competitor offers
today. The nearest competitors are broad, English-only, tracker-focused platforms with generic nationwide communities. The Village wins on locality,
trust, language, and practical utility.

POSITIONING STATEMENT & MESSAGING GUIDE
The Village Positioning

For mothers in Miami navigating pregnancy and early motherhood, The Village is the first bilingual, local-first community app that connects you to trusted
specialists, real-time events, peer support, and AI-powered guidance — all in one place. Unlike Peanut or BabyCenter, The Village was built for your city,
your language, and your community.
