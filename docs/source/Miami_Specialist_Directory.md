    🍼 Breast Milk — Donation, Sharing & Community
     ⚠ Safety Note for The Village App



     Always direct mothers to certified milk banks first. Peer-to-peer sharing carries health risks — the FDA and AAP
     recommend pasteurized donor milk from accredited banks. Present community platforms as secondary options
     with clear safety guidance.
